# Canvasify — Master Plan

## Vision
Enable anyone in the world to transform their most cherished memories into personalized creative experiences using AI.

## Mission
Build the world's leading platform for AI-powered personalized art — starting with paint-by-number, expanding into a full creative commerce ecosystem.

## Tagline
**Turn Memories Into Masterpieces**

---

## The Problem
- 10,000+ photos sit locked on every person's phone, slowly losing emotional value
- Generic craft store paint-by-number kits have zero personal meaning
- Professional custom art costs ₹10,000–₹50,000 and takes weeks
- No product exists that makes personalized art accessible, affordable, and instant

## The Solution
Upload any photo → AI converts it to a numbered template → receive a professional canvas kit at your door. Results in under 5 minutes. From ₹499.

## Target Customers

### Primary: The Memory Keeper (25–45F, tier 1/2 cities)
- Milestone events: weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, new pets
- Gifting occasions: Mother's Day, Diwali, Christmas
- Values: emotional connection, uniqueness, quality

### Secondary: The Corporate Buyer (HR, marketing teams)
- Employee gifts, client appreciation, team building
- Orders 10–500 units, needs bulk pricing + custom packaging

### Tertiary: The Creative Hobbyist (35–60, all genders)
- Existing interest in painting and craft
- Finds generic kits boring, wants a personal challenge
